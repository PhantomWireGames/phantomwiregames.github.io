About the Game

Race. Draft.
Survive.

Nightshift drops you into an underground elimination circuit ranked by 1000 drivers. You start dead last. The city is procedurally generated every run - every route, every corner, every shortcut is different.

Between races, a perk draft system lets you shape your playstyle run by run. Go wide and aggressive, or surgical and precise - the meta is yours to build.

Six race event types. Thirty-one vehicles across three tiers. AI racers with their own tactics and aggression profiles. Every night is different. Every run pushes you closer to the top - or ends your streak.

Key Features
Elimination Tournament
1000 ranked racers. Every race cuts the bottom placers. Rivals keep climbing in the background. Constant pressure, emergent rivalries, natural stakes.
Perk Draft System
After each race, pick 1 of 3 random perks across 4 rarities - Common to Legendary. Build drift, NOS, combat, or slipstream-focused runs. No two runs play the same.
In-Race Pickups
NOS Burst, EMP Pulse, Oil Slick, Shockwave, Repair Kit, Spike Strip and more. Active collectibles that add chaos and create combat racing moments mid-race.
Named Rival Bosses
Every 100 ranks, face a legend in a high-stakes 1v1 duel. The Wraith (#900), Voltage (#700), King Static (#500), Reiko (#300), Phantom (#100), Nightshift (#1).
Chaos Vehicle Physics
UE5 Chaos system with fluid semi-realistic arcade handling. Cel-shaded visuals inspired by NFS Underground, Tokyo Xtreme Racer, and Initial D.
