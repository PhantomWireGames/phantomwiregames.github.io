Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Who We Are

PhantomWire is an independent game studio based in the United Kingdom. Our website is phantomwiregames.com. You can contact us at hello@phantomwiregames.com.

What Data We Collect

This website does not collect personal data by default. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising networks, or analytics without your consent.

If you contact us via email or a contact form, we will receive your name and email address solely for the purpose of responding to your enquiry. This data is not shared with third parties.

Cookies

We use a single functional cookie to remember your cookie consent preference. This cookie does not track you and contains no personal information. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's local storage.

If you accept analytics cookies in the consent banner, we may use a privacy-respecting analytics tool (such as Plausible) to understand general traffic patterns. No personal data is collected or shared in this process.

Third Party Services

Our game pages may embed content from Steam (Valve Corporation). Steam's own privacy policy governs any data collected by their embedded widgets. We recommend reviewing Steam's Privacy Policy if you have concerns.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold about you. As we collect very little data, this is unlikely to be necessary — but if you have any concerns, contact us at hello@phantomwiregames.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the studio grows and new services are added. Any significant changes will be noted at the top of this page with an updated date.
