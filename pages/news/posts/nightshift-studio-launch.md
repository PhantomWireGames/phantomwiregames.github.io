**PhantomWire is a new independent game studio based in the UK.** We're building our first title - Nightshift, a roguelite street racing tournament set in the world of 90s and early 00s underground racing culture. This post is about what that means, what we're building, and where we are right now.

## What is Nightshift?

Nightshift drops you into an underground elimination circuit ranked by 1000 drivers. You start dead last at rank #1000. The goal is simple - reach the top. But the circuit is procedurally assembled every run, perks change your build each time, and every 100 ranks a named rival stands between you and the next tier.

It's a roguelite first. The racing is the vehicle for a progression loop built around risk, reward, and building a run identity through the perk draft system. Think Hades meets Need for Speed Underground.

## Where are we right now?

Early development. The core systems are being built out - Chaos Vehicle physics in UE5, the PCGEx-powered city and route generation, the tournament rank structure, and the perk draft system. It's not pretty yet. That's fine. We're building the foundation right before anything else.

Devlog posts will follow as systems get built. Screenshots and video when there's something worth showing.

## What's coming next?

The next post will cover the core loop in detail - how the elimination tournament, perk draft, and procedural routes combine to create a run-based racing game that feels different every time. After that, PCG city generation, the rival system, and eventually a demo.
